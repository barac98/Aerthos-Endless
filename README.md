# Aerthos: Endless Ascent

**Aerthos: Endless Ascent** is a Gothic-themed TCG Idle RPG where you command a team of powerful Paragons to climb the infinite Tower of Sundering. Balance short-term gains with long-term power as you navigate the tower's obsidian heights.

## ⚔️ Core Gameplay

*   **Infinite Ascent**: Battle through endless floors of increasingly powerful enemies. Each floor defeated grants Gold and Soul Shards.
*   **Real-Time Combat**: Watch your team of up to 5 Paragons unleash attacks and special abilities. Track your DPS and critical hits in real-time.
*   **Boss Encounters**: Every 10 floors, face a powerful boss that tests your team's strength and strategy.

## 🛡️ The Paragon System

Recruit and level up legendary heroes from the Silver Woods and the Shadow Realms:
*   **Kaelen Bold**: A fallen knight whose damage scales with floor height.
*   **Silas Vane**: An ancient vampire specializing in % Max HP damage against bosses.
*   **Elara**: A swift elven archer with high critical rate and attack speed.
*   **Oghul**: A mountain-born giant delivering massive AoE ground-slams.

## 📈 Progression & Upgrades

### Battle Training (Temporal)
Hone your skills for the current ascent. These upgrades are powerful but are lost to the Sundering:
*   **Attack**: Increases base damage per level.
*   **Speed**: Faster attack timers for all Paragons.
*   **Crit**: Higher chance for devastating critical strikes.
*   **Greed**: Increases Gold drops from defeated enemies.

### Runic Altar (Permanent)
Sunder your progress to claim the **Essence of the Tower**. Use Essence at the Altar for permanent, multiplicative bonuses:
*   **Attack Mult**: Multiplies all damage dealt.
*   **Gold Mult**: Permanent boost to gold acquisition.
*   **Shard Mult**: Increases Soul Shard drop rates.
*   **Essence Gain**: Boosts the amount of Essence earned per Sundering.
*   **Crit Rate**: Permanent addition to team-wide critical chance.
*   **Speed Mult**: Permanent multiplier for attack speed.

## 🌀 Sundering Reset
The ultimate sacrifice. Perform a Sundering Reset to return to Floor 1 and wipe your temporal training. In exchange, you gain **Essence** based on your highest floor reached, allowing you to grow stronger for the next ascent.

## 📱 Technical Features

*   **Responsive Design**: Optimized grid layouts for mobile, tablet, and desktop screens.
*   **Persistence**: Your progress is automatically saved using IndexedDB (via LocalForage).
*   **Dynamic Animations**: Smooth transitions and combat effects powered by Framer Motion.
*   **State Management**: Robust game logic handled by Zustand.
